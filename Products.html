Fair-trade, sustainably sourced goods.

Here at Chambers Coffee Co. we believe everyone is equal and thats why we only use suppliers that can provide a papertrail of wages leading back to the farmer that produced the good.

In order to maintain our standards for high quality Coffee's we evaluate our producers against the following standards;

  • Quality Requirment- All the produce we sell must meet the Chambers Coffee Co. high stnadards.
  • Economic Accountability- There must be evidence to show how much Chambers Coffee Co. pays actually gets to the farmer.
  • Social Responsibility- Protects workers rights, ensuring safe humane working conditions. Compliance with minimum wage requirements and addressing issues such as child/forced labour.
  • Environmental Concern- Measurements to help manage waste, conserve natural resources and preserve biodiversity.
